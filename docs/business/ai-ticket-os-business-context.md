# AI Ticket OS Business Context

## Positioning

AI Ticket OS is an enterprise AI customer service operating system for large organizations that run customer service centers, call centers, after-sales centers, and customer success operations.

The product blends operating patterns inspired by OpenAI, Linear, Jira, Salesforce, Zendesk, ServiceNow, HubSpot, Datadog, and Notion.

## Core Jobs

- Operate omnichannel customer service from one command center.
- Manage tickets, calls, live chats, email, SMS, and social channels.
- Assist agents with AI recommendations, real-time copilots, knowledge retrieval, and workflow automation.
- Monitor SLA, quality, risk, sentiment, performance, and operational health.
- Govern enterprise access with RBAC, data permissions, audit logs, and tenant controls.

## Enterprise Users

- Support Agent: handles assigned tickets, calls, chats, and replies.
- Team Lead: monitors queue pressure, SLA risk, and agent performance.
- Operations Manager: owns service health, workforce scheduling, quality, and escalations.
- AI Operations Lead: manages agents, prompts, workflows, models, token cost, and evaluation.
- Knowledge Manager: manages FAQ, articles, RAG configuration, vector stores, and knowledge review.
- Security Administrator: manages RBAC, audit, tenant settings, API permissions, and integrations.
- Executive: consumes CEO, operations, support, and AI operation cockpits.
